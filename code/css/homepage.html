    Frequently Asked Questions: Cloud Storage

    What is Cloud Storage?
    • It is a type of computer data storage where digital data is stored on a server in off-site locations. Cloud Storage enables any business to store, access, and maintain data.
    • Cloud Storage Services takes responsibility for hosting, managing, and securing the data stored in the infrastructure. In addition, the provider ensures that the data is visible and accessible via a public or private Internet connection.
    How does Cloud Storage work?
    • It uses remote servers to store data such as files, videos, images, etc. When users upload their data through an internet connection, it will be stored on a virtual machine on a physical server.
    • The Cloud Storage provider will create a new virtual server to meet the demand for growing storage needs. This allows users to access files anytime and anywhere.
    Why should I use eWallHost as a Cloud Storage service?
    Whether you are an individual o a business owner, eWallHost guarantees a secure data storage solution. So, you can store, share, and edit your entire data without worrying about security or costs. Our experienced technical team is here to handle all your queries and ensure that you get the most secure and satisfactory Cloud Storage services.
    What are the advantages of Cloud Storage?
    • Some of the advantages of Cloud Storage are that you can access your files from anywhere with a continuous internet connection, and it acts as a backup solution for your local storage on physical drives.
    • If you are buying these Cloud Storage services for your business, it is ideal to collaborate on documents and files. In addition, it is highly scalable, allowing you to expand or reduce data according to your needs.
    How much storage space is available in the cloud?
    eWallHost Cloud Storage will ofer you 1 TB of storage; if you need more storage, you can pay extra and enjoy hassle-free storage.
    What are the different protocols that can be used to access a storage box?
    Protocols such as FTP, SFTP, SC, and RSYNC will allow you to access and manage your storage box. In addition, you can easily integrate them into your own file system through WebDAV or Samba.
